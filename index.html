Jellyfish are awesome!

Did you know jellyfish are the oldest multicellular animals on the planet?

Jellyfish Facts

A group of jellyfish can be called a bloom, a swarm or a smack.

Another name for an adult jellyfish is a Medusa.

Baby jellyfish are called 'ephyrae'.

Jellyfish are a type of plankton, which comes from the Greek word meaning "to drift".
